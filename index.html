Codeday Mentor Guide

Your Goals

  • Work with students to help them complete their projects.
  • Solve problems so that they can keep programming.
  • Bring resources to students so that they can finish up what they need.
  • Advise them on ways to manage their time, and how to build a successful project.
  • Show students what working in our industry is like.
  • Ensure a safe environment for students to learn and thrive.




Menotoring

  • Ask open ended questions that help students solve their problems.
  • Do more listening then talking.
  • Treat students as peers and not children.
  • Be ok not knowing how to answer a question.
  • Patience is the most important thing you can give to someone learning. Listen and give full attention to whom you are talking to.




Event Rules

  • Students are not allowed to leave the location without their parents checking them out. If you see a student leaving please inform Kristine Romaine as soon as possible.
  • Students can move items around the location as needed, and sit anywhere that they find space. Out of bounds areas are marked by caution tape.
  • If you see someone that doesn't look like they belong at the event please inform the security members at UAT.
